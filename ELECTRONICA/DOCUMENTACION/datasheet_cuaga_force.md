## ICSP

To load code to the board.

* MISO - PB2
* MOSI - PB1
* SCK - PC1
* RESRT - PC3

## I2C

For master slave communication with mega.

* SDA - PB1
* SDL - PC1
* HANDSHAKE - PB3

### DIRECTION

* PA1
* PA2

## ENCODERS

* INT1 - PB0
* INT2 - PC2

## DRIVER

* PWM - PA5
* ENABLE - PA3
* CS - PA4
* INA - PA6
* INB - PA7

## LEDS

* LED1 - PA0
* LED2 - PC0
